## CLC Tennis Video Editing Rules

### Framing for 9:16 Vertical
- Source footage is 16:9 (2560x1440). Output is 9:16 (1080x1920).
- The full height of the source is visible — banners, sky, and court all show.
- ALWAYS zoom in 1.6x+ to focus on court-level action. Players are in the lower 60% of the frame.
- Use `transformOrigin: 'center 65%'` to anchor zoom on the court, not the sky.

### Clip Selection Priority
1. Kids actively hitting the ball (rallies, serves, volleys) — BEST content
2. Coach-player interaction (drills, instruction) — HIGH value
3. Group activity on court (warmups, games) — GOOD
4. Establishing shots of venue — use sparingly (1 clip max)
5. Spectators/crowds — AVOID unless needed for context

### Timestamp Scores Interpretation
- `tennis=5, movement=5`: Peak gameplay — use for hooks and peaks
- `tennis=4, movement=4`: Active drills/practice — good for build sections
- `tennis=2-3, people=4-5`: Spectators watching — NOT gameplay, avoid for action clips
- `tennis=1, movement=1`: Empty court/ground — NEVER use

### Mode-Specific Structure
**Game Day (30s TikTok):**
- [0-3s] HOOK — highest tennis+movement score moment
- [3-12s] BUILD — 2-3 clips of increasing action
- [12-18s] PEAK — best gameplay moment, consider 0.5x slow-mo
- [18-25s] ENERGY — diverse angles, different players
- [25-30s] CTA — branding/website

**Our Story (60-90s):**
- [0-5s] Cold open — strongest visual
- [5-20s] Context — venue, atmosphere
- [20-60s] The story — coaching, gameplay, community
- [60-80s] Emotional peak
- [80-90s] Resolution + CTA

### Text Overlay Rules
- First text appears at 0s to stop the scroll
- Use location names (Hempstead, Brooklyn, US Open) for authenticity
- CTA card at the end with communityliteracyclub.org
- Font: bold, uppercase for game_day; mixed case for our_story
- Text position: bottom third to not obscure players
