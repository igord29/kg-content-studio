## Platform Specifications

### TikTok / Instagram Reels (9:16)
- Resolution: 1080x1920
- Duration: 15-60s (sweet spot: 27-33s)
- Hook MUST be in first 1-2 seconds
- Fast cuts: 2-4s per clip for game_day
- Captions: large, centered, bold — assume sound-off viewing
- No letterboxing — fill the entire frame
- Music tier 1: export with ambient audio, add trending sound at upload

### Instagram Feed (1:1)
- Resolution: 1080x1080
- Duration: 15-60s
- Wider framing — can show more of the court
- Lower third text overlays

### YouTube Shorts (9:16)
- Resolution: 1080x1920
- Duration: up to 60s
- Similar to TikTok but can be slightly longer
- End with subscribe CTA

### LinkedIn / Facebook (16:9)
- Resolution: 1920x1080
- Duration: 30-120s
- Slower pacing, more context
- Subtitles essential (autoplay is muted)

## Universal Rules
- First frame must have text or strong visual — never start with black/empty
- End with clear CTA (website, follow, subscribe)
- Color grade: warm, slightly saturated for youth sports
- Transitions: fast cuts for energy content, dissolves for story content
